About Us

Who are we?

DUYGU TEXTILE produces for the world's leading fashion and store chains with its 30 years of experience. The company, which exports 80% of its production, offers its own collections to its customers with its strong design and technical team and meets the collection demands of its customers.

The company, which produces in accordance with BSCI and SEDEX standards, which are valid in the international field, serves its customers with a capacity of 150,000 / month with its state-of-the-art knitting and sewing machines.

DUYGU TEXTILE has taken its place among the preferred manufacturers in the knitwear production of world famous brands with its quality, fast service and right price policies.

Corporate

Duygu Textile continues to specialize in print and plain dyed product groups, especially in women's and men's accessories, by continuing its production for many world brands such as scarves, shawls, kerchiefs, bandanas, handkerchiefs and hair accessories.

Duygu Textile has the production track and experience that can turn the fabric from yarn, which is the raw material, into the final product, thanks to its production capability and organization. In addition, new collections are offered to customers by the design and pattern team.

Duygu Textile derives its strength from the quality and fast services it provides to its customers. Efforts continue to focus on the needs of customers and to offer a best-in-class approach.

Moving on to become a strong brand with the vision of fully meeting the needs of the customers / companies and brands that are in cooperation, Duygu Textile has taken the principle of quality products and customer satisfaction one step further with the power it has received from its patented production techniques and vertical integration; focused on developing new products and coming to the forefront with its innovative approach in printing and flat dyeing areas.

Mission

We aim to provide quality service in the textile sector with our young, dynamic and expert staff by following the production technologies closely and constantly improving in line with the changing consumer needs and fashion understanding.

Vision

It is one of our primary visions to be among the leading companies of the textile industry worldwide, in the light of our values, with the effort of our team to reach high living standards and to be an exemplary institution that respects nature.
